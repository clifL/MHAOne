Frequently Asked Question (FAQ)

What are the Operating Hours?

Our operating hours are from 9am to 6pm daily.

I have booked an appointment. Do I need to reach the Medical Center early?

Yes. You are required to reach the medical center 15 minutes before your appointment to complete the registration

How do I make book an appointment in advance?

Hover over to the dropdown menu and click on "Appointment". Choose between consultation and vaccination. Afterwards, you would be redirected to an appointment summary page where the option to "Make New Appointment" is shown. Click on the option and proceed from there.

What do I need to know before booking an appointment?

You will need to know the pupose of visit, if you are going for vaccination or to consult a doctor.

Why is my view queue empty when I select view queue in the menu?

You may not have anything in your View Queue cause you have not done your registration. To complete it, please click on the Triple Lines icon located at the top left of the screen and select Walk-in / Check-in.
